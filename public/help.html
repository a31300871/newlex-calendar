How to Use Near and Far Events

A quick guide to finding what's happening in your community — and getting your own event, business, or sponsorship out there.

1 Browsing events near you

When you land on the Calendar, you'll see events posted for your area. Each event shows up as one tappable line with the name, location, time, and price. Tap any event to see the full details — description, who posted it, and a button to add it to your own calendar app.

📱 On mobile? The whole page is built to work on a phone. The filter pills at the top stay visible while you scroll — no need to scroll back up.

2 Filtering by area, date, type, and "Free"

Three filter pills sit at the top of the calendar:

  • 📍 Area — your zipcode. Tap to change it. You can also click 🗺️ Browse by State / Map to pick a state and see popular zipcodes there.
  • 📅 When — tap to choose Today, This Week, This Month, or All. You can also tap a specific date (or two dates for a range) on the mini-calendar.
  • 🏷️ Type — pick one or more event categories (Sports, Government, Community, etc.). Includes a special 🆓 Free events only filter at the top.
💡 Tip: Filters combine — picking "Free events only" + "Community" shows only free community events. Tap "All types" to clear them.

3 Using the search bar (state-wide option)

The search bar matches event names, locations, and categories. Type, press Enter, and results filter live.

Next to the search bar is a small 🌎 State checkbox. Tick it to expand your search across every zipcode in your state — useful for finding state fairs, regional festivals, or anything that draws people from far away.

4 Submitting an event (account or guest)

Tap the big + ADD EVENT button. You have two paths:

  1. Sign in or register — events you post go live faster, you can edit/delete them later, and your account is yours forever.
  2. Submit as a guest ✨ — no account needed. We'll email you a confirmation link (so we know it's a real submission) and a separate "magic link" to edit or delete your event later. Save that email!

Fill in the event name, date, location, optional time, optional description, and an optional price (with a Free quick-fill button). Hit Enter or tap Submit Event.

🌎 Going state-wide? If your event covers the whole state (state fair, tournament finals), tick the "Make this event state-wide" box. It'll need admin approval before going live in every zip.

5 Finding local businesses

The Directory tab shows businesses in your area. The top section is reserved for ⭐ Featured Businesses (paid spots), followed by all free community listings below.

Filter by category (food, retail, services, etc.) or search by name. Each listing has the business name, category, phone, website, and address if provided.

6 Listing your own business — free

Click the + List My Business button on the Directory page. Free for any local business. You provide:

  • Business name + category
  • Phone, website, address (any combination)
  • Short description
  • Your zipcode (so it appears in the right area)

That's it. Your business appears in the directory of every visitor who matches your zip.

7 Buying an advertisement

Three separate ad tiers, billed monthly through Stripe — pick what fits your goal:

  • 📢 Sponsor Bar — $25/mo: always-visible card pinned at the bottom of every page
  • ⭐ Featured Directory — $30/mo: top placement on the directory page with a gradient-bordered card
  • 🎯 Event Popup Ad — $50/mo: sponsored card inside every event detail popup (limited to 5 spots per area)

Go to the Advertise page, pick your tier, register, and check out via Stripe. Each ad is zipcode-bound — your ad only shows to visitors viewing your target zipcode.

8 Sponsoring the calendar

Spot the 💛 sponsor card at the top of the events list? That's our calendar sponsor program. For any amount you choose, your name (or business, or tribute, or "anonymous") appears on the calendar for two weeks as a thank-you.

Pick from five display options:

  • Completely anonymous
  • First name only
  • First and last name
  • Business name + website/phone
  • In memory / In honor of someone
⚖️ Important: Calendar sponsorships are paid promotional placements, not tax-deductible donations. Payments are non-refundable. You must be 18 or older.

9 Accounts, sign-in, and managing your stuff

The Register or Sign In pill is in the top-right of every page. Once signed in:

  • The pill shows your initials + name
  • Click the down-arrow next to your name to open the account menu
  • From there you can change your password, download your data, or delete your account entirely

Accounts are optional for browsing and event submission, but recommended if you'll be posting regularly.
